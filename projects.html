Located on the southern coast of Rio de Janeiro, the Laranjeiras Residence is inspired by the exuberant nature of the surroundings. The steel is the structural core, the wood the shell that protects it from the sea and connects it with the green area, one of the most beautiful in Brazil.

CAPSULA TINY HOMES (Netherlands)


Tiny Holiday Home, is a wonderful little house that can easily accommodate a family of four, includes a living room, a kitchen/dining room, a patio, no less than three bedrooms, a bathroom and a toilet.
